My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2023

Game Modification: A War of Suits
This game modification assignment was about fixing a broken game. A War of Suits is a revamped version of the classic card game, War. Two players will lead an army of either the red or black cards to fight their opponent. On each turn, players will send one of their cards into battle, hoping to beat the value of their opponent’s card. There are many twists and turns when doubles are played against one another, or when an ace shows up to reverse the course of action. The winner of the game is determined by the total score of the players’ victory piles calculated at the end.

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: On the Lam!
On The Lam! is a strategic board game based off of film noir movies from the 1940's. The players are all suspects of a crime committed in Rion City, and they are now on the lam! The player’s goal is to be the first suspect to collect five of their character’s alibi cards, and take them to the police station to prove their innocence.

Interactive Fiction: Ice Cream Escapade
Made with Inform, Ice Cream Escapade is a short story where you play a child who wakes up in the middle of the night, wanting to go down to the kitchen and get some ice cream. But, they are afraid of the dark and monsters that could be in the closet, so you must find your way around these obstacles to progress through the story. (Currently, game does not go further than the hallway outside of the bedroom).

Hypertext Fiction: What's In The Attic?
Made in Twine, What's In The Attic is an interactive story with horror elements, where the player has moved into a new house, but something unknown is making noises in the attic. You must explore the house to find the code that unlocks the attic hatch, and see what awaits you up there.
PLAY
Visual Novel: On the Train to London
Made with Renpy, On the Train to London is a game that follows the story of Gwendoline Davies, a young farm girl from Derbyshire England in 1901. She is taking a train to London and meets some interesting characters along the way. Depending on how she interacts with her fellow train riders, it may determine her future living in London. (Only 2 NPCs currently in the game).
